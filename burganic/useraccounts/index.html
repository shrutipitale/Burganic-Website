    Personal Hygiene & Food Preparation with concern to food safety

    We wear suitable hygienic clothes, including gloves, hair covering, footwear, where necessary.

    we prevent contamination of ready-to-eat foods from cutting boards, utensils, clothing, raw meat or eggs.

    we do not touch ready-to-eat foods with bare hands.

    They do not wear watches or jewellery.

    They Wash hands, especially after handling raw meat, before and after wearing gloves, going to the toilet, handling waste, after cleaning, blowing nose, and after touching phones, light switches, door handles or money.

    They dry their hands using a disposable towel.

    They do not work in a food handling area if ill with diarrhoea, vomiting, infectious disease or have open wounds or skin infections.

    We use safe water in food preparation and for washing fruits and vegetables to be eaten raw.

    We Cook food at the required temperature.

    They do not smoke, spit, sneeze, touch face or hair, or eat food, while handling food


    Our Chefs their cover hair while cooking food.
